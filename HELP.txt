Run "make" in order to make an executable of the game
Also you should have OpenGl installed on your computer

Controls -

'B' or 'b' selects the cannon
'R' or 'r' selects the red basket
'G' or 'g' selects the green basket
Left and Right arrow keys translate the selcted object left and rigtht respectively
Up and Down arrow keys rotate the cannon left and right when it is selected
Spacebar shoots lasers at intervals on 1 second when cannon is selected
Mouse left click can be used to select and drag objects
Mouse right click can be used to rotate the cannon
'P' or 'p' pauses the game
'Q' or 'q' or 'ESC' to keys exit the game
